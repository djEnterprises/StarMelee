HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE: Win 2 of 3 matches by destroying your opponent's ship.

MATCH FLOW: Each match starts with a 10-second practice countdown (no specials, no power-ups). Then 2 minutes of combat. Last ship standing wins. If time runs out, ship with more health wins.

CONTROLS (Sega Genesis layout):

  • A = Primary weapon (hold for autofire)
  • B = Secondary weapon (hold for autofire)
  • C = Special weapon (long cooldown)
  • X = Accelerate
  • Y = Brake
  • Z = Speed Boost
  • A+B = Transporter Beam → Quantum Torpedo
  • B+C = Cloaking Device
  • A+C = Self-Destruct (4-sec timer, kills you + enemy)

METERS: Life (green→red), Shield (blue), Battery (purple). Shields must be DOWN to use Transporter Beam.

POWER-UPS: Floating icons in the arena. Fly into them. Restore life/shield/battery, gain torpedoes, extend timer.

FATALITY: Win via Quantum Torpedo and you'll see the FATALITY screen.
